3. What Data We Collect

  • Authorities, if legally required

We use cookies to enhance functionality and collect anonymized data. Details can be found in our Cookie Policy. Analytics tools may transfer data to third countries (e.g., USA), based on your consent.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the stated purposes or legal obligations.

9. Your Rights

  • Access (Art. 15 GDPR)

  • Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)

We employ technical and organizational security measures to protect your data from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. Please check regularly for updates.
