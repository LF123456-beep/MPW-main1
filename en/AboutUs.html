Muniqo: Our Journey

2023

Founding idea, first awards and competition successes.

More Info

Luca and Sebastian met at TU Munich and recognized the enormous potential of active vibration damping for medical transport. First proof-of-concepts were developed and the TUM IDEAward 2023 was won.

2024

Market validation, first real pilot and EXIST funding.

More Info

In early 2024, Luca and Sebastian applied to the XPLORE program. Upon acceptance, they expanded the team, conducted market analyses, identified neonatal transport as the primary target market, and launched a realistic pilot project with Munich Fire Department, Heart Center, and neonatology experts. Over 10 Letters of Intent confirmed broad interest. EXIST funding was approved.

2025

Full-time development, ESA BIC funding and growing strategic partnerships.

More Info

After completing their studies, Luca and Sebastian started full-time at Muniqo. Admission to the ESA BIC program, team expansion, and pilot project phase 2. First place at the Munich Impact Incubator, finalist at TUM Pitch Perfect and Generation D.

2026

From pilot to certification, first clinical installations and initial sales.

More Info

By mid-2026, product development will be completed, Phase 3 validates the system in neonatal helicopter transport. CE marking (MDR risk class I) and patent finalized. First installations and sales in the second half of the year.

2030+

Standard for critical transport and expansion into new billion-dollar markets.

More Info

By 2030, Muniqo will be established as the standard solution for vibration-free patient transport. Over 500 systems installed. Expansion into vibration diagnostics. Target: healthcare market > €350M, multi-billion-dollar markets and measurable impact — saving lives.
